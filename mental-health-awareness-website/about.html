About Me

I am a licensed clinical psychologist with a deep passion for mental health advocacy and technology. My journey began with a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from Stanford University, where I specialized in cognitive-behavioral therapy and digital mental health interventions.

Over the years, I've combined my clinical expertise with web development skills to create innovative mental health platforms and resources. I believe that technology can be a powerful tool for making mental health support more accessible, especially for underserved communities.

My work focuses on developing evidence-based digital interventions, creating user-friendly mental health applications, and advocating for mental health awareness through technology. I'm committed to using my skills to make a positive impact on people's lives.
